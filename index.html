Milestones in Technology
In 1945 John Van Neumann writes first draft of a report on the EDVAC. EDVAC

ERA 1101 introduced

In 1950 one of the first commercially produced computers, the company´s first customer was the US Navy. ERA 1101

Amdahl Corporation introduces the Amdahl 470

In 1970 470V/6 was the company’s first product and ran the same software as IBM System/370 computers but cost less and was smaller and faster. Amdahl 470

68000 Microprocessor

In 1980 Motorola introduces the 68000 Microprocessor. This high performance processor found its place in powerful work stations intended for graphics-intensive programs common in engineering. 68000 Microprocessor

Macintosh Portable

In 1990 Macintosh Portable is introduced. The Macintosh Portable was heavy, weighing sixteen pounds, and expensive (US$6,500). Macintosh Portable
